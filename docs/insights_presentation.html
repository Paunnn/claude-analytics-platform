Three things worth acting on
Finding 1 → Action
Set token alerts for the 9 Power Users
Three engineers hit 290K+ tokens in a single day during January. A simple alert at 150K tokens/day adds zero friction but gives engineering managers visibility before a session inflates monthly spend. The top 5 users already account for 20% of total cost.
Cost control
Finding 2 → Action
Find out why engineers code on Sundays
Sunday is the single busiest day (17.9% of requests). That is unusual and could mean voluntary side projects, deadline pressure, or timezone distribution effects. A short anonymous survey would clarify this — the right management response depends entirely on the answer.
People & culture
Finding 3 → Action
Audit Bash failures — 3,006 failed commands per quarter
Bash is the second most-used tool and the only one below 95% success (93.1%). Pulling error logs and categorising the top failure types would likely surface a small number of fixable patterns — environment issues, path problems, or specific command patterns the model gets wrong.
